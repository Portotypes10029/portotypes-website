About Us

We are a rookie team (2025) based at Portage High School in Portage, Indiana. We are part of FIRST Indiana Robotics and compete in district events. We are a passionate team of high school students dedicated to designing, building, and programming competitive robots. Our mission is to learn, innovate, and compete at the highest levels of robotics competitions.
